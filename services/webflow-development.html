Our Webflow Development Process

We’ve refined our process to be efficient, predictable, and focused on what matters most: delivering a high-quality Webflow site on time and on budget.

Step 1
Briefing & Planning
Our partnership begins with a complimentary strategy call to discuss your project goals and review your existing designs (e.g., Figma, Sketch, XD). Afterward, we provide a detailed project plan and a transparent estimate based on our hourly rates, giving you a clear projection of the investment required - with no commitment.
Step 2
Project Start & Setup
Once you approve the estimate, we officially kick off the project. We'll set up a shared communication channel (like Slack) and a project management board. You'll provide us with all the necessary assets: final design files, brand guidelines, copy, and any required account access.
Step 3
Webflow Development
Our developers begin building your website in Webflow, following best practices for clean, scalable, and SEO-friendly code. We develop on a private staging link, giving you real-time access to see the progress as we translate your designs into a fully functional and responsive website.
Step 4
Quality Assurance & Revisions
Before handover, our team conducts thorough quality assurance (QA) testing to ensure everything is pixel-perfect and bug-free across all devices and browsers. We then invite your team to review the staging site and provide feedback for a final round of revisions and refinements.
Step 5
Launch & Training
Upon your final approval, we handle the entire technical launch process for a smooth go-live. To empower your team, we deliver custom video tutorials that show you exactly how to manage content on your new website, ensuring you have full control from day one.
